Welcome to Mood Moves
No matter how you feel, you still train. Just differently.

Some days you give 100%. Some days you give 10%. Both count. Both build you.

Mood Moves was built for those days too — because a 10% workout still beats zero. Every single time.

"Just meet yourself where you are. We'll take it from there."

No guilt. No pressure. Just movement that works for YOU.

Here is how it works

You pick your goal and fitness level. We build your plan.
Every day you tell us how you feel — we do the rest:

😞 Low energy → gentle & light
😤 Angry → intense & powerful
😊 Happy → full energy, full effort
😵‍💫 Stressed → rhythmic & calming
😐 Neutral → steady & strong

Same goal. Different workout every day — because YOU are different every day.
