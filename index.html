Welcome to Mood Moves

Every emotion carries energy. We help you turn that energy into movement that matters.

Some days you give 100%. Some days you give 10%. And both of those days count. Both build you. Mood Moves was made for the 10% days just as much as the 100% ones — because showing up, even gently, still beats staying still. Every single time.

Sad, angry, happy, stressed — whatever you bring through the door, we work with it. Your mood isn't an obstacle. It's the plan. No guilt. No pressure. Just movement that meets you exactly where you are.

"Just meet yourself where you are.
We'll take it from there."

What we collect

We collect your name, email address, fitness goals, workout history, journal entries, and progress photos you choose to upload. All data is stored securely using Supabase.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to personalise your workout experience. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

Photos

Progress photos are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly share them.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use basic analytics to understand how the app is used. No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics providers.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. You can also clear your data directly in the app via Profile settings.
